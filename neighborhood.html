Neighborhood

Nowadays, Casey lives in the foothills of Provo, Utah. Provo sits in the southern end of Utah, County. It is best known for being the home of Brigham Young University. The foothills are the part of Provo closest to its mountains. Notably, the foothills neighborhood is underneath Brigham Young University's iconic "Y" on Y-Mountain.
